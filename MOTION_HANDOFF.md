# Uniun Motion Prototype Handoff

This prototype is a Next.js app-router project using Yarn. It covers the Phase 1 and Phase 2 motion pass: layout, story order, pinned sections, sticky phone transitions, floating notes, graph lines, and ownership CTA.

## Story Arc

1. One note appears.
2. One note becomes a network.
3. Notes become feed, channels, threads, graph, Shiv, and conversation tree.
4. Create, organize, reflect become Brahma, Vishnu, Shiv.
5. Shiv wakes up from connected notes.
6. Ownership closes the emotional arc.
7. Final CTA starts again from one note.

## Figma Frame Plan

- `01 Cinematic Hero`: dark pinned scene, one note, network lines, phone reveal.
- `02 Note Forms`: warm white explanation grid.
- `03 Sticky Product Journey`: fixed phone with six screen states.
- `04 Brahma Vishnu Shiv`: three-part transition with central note.
- `05 Teams Communities Movements`: cluster graph visual.
- `06 Shiv AI`: notes flowing into AI orb and response card.
- `07 Ownership Manifesto`: mature, high-contrast trust cards.
- `08 Final Download`: graph resolving into Uniun logo and platform CTAs.
